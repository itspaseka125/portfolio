How I work

No mystery,
no surprises.

Every project follows the same four-step rhythm. You get visibility at every stage, not just when the doc lands in your inbox.

01
Brief & discovery

I define the product, audience, and conversion goal. This includes understanding what's being sold, who it's for, and what success looks like.

02

I use AI tools to accelerate research across reviews, Reddit, competitor pages, and sales feedback to extract real customer pain points and language.
